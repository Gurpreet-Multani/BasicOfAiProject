Introduction

Welcome to the Basic of AI Project repository! This project serves as an introductory educational resource designed to demystify the core concepts and fundamental algorithms that form the foundation of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Whether you are a beginner looking to understand what AI is, a student seeking clear explanations of key terms, or a developer wanting a quick refresher on foundational concepts, this resource is for you.
